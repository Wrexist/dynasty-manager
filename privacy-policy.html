Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Dynasty Manager ("the App") is developed by Dynasty Manager ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Game Data: All game saves, settings, and progress are stored locally on your device using browser or app storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store your game data on any server.

Purchase Data: If you make in-app purchases, transaction processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) and our payment partner RevenueCat. We receive anonymized purchase receipts to unlock features but do not store your payment details.

Advertising Data: If you choose to watch rewarded ads, our advertising partner Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy. Ad viewing is entirely opt-in.

Crash Reports: We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and error diagnostics to improve app stability. These reports may include device type, OS version, and stack traces but do not contain personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App
  • To process in-app purchases and restore entitlements
  • To display opt-in rewarded advertisements
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited data only with:

  • RevenueCat — for purchase processing and subscription management
  • Google AdMob — for serving opt-in rewarded ads
  • Sentry — for crash reporting and error diagnostics

4. Data Storage & Security

Game data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data. Purchase entitlements are managed by RevenueCat and the respective app stores.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your Rights

Since game data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all app data at any time through your device settings or the in-app "Reset Game" option. For purchase-related data, contact Apple or Google through their respective support channels.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
