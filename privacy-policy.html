Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Introduction

Dynasty Manager ("we", "our", or "the app") is a single-player football management simulation game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Dynasty Manager does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline on your device.

Local Storage

The app saves your game progress locally on your device using your browser's or app's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection tools.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: dynastymanager@proton.me
